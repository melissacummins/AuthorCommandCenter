# Author Ad Copy Pro

Write high-converting Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ad copy for your romance books. This plugin
reads your manuscript, builds reader avatar profiles, finds your strongest hooks, and produces
paste-ready ad copy optimized for Meta's Andromeda algorithm.

## What's Inside

### Skills

- **Ad Copy Writer** — The core skill. Uses your Book Analysis (Story Overview, avatars, hooks)
  to write complete paste-ready ad copy: 5 primary text variations, 5 headlines, a 4-paragraph
  SEO description block, reel scripts, and creative direction with AI image generation prompts.

- **Avatar Builder** — Builds detailed reader persona profiles from your manuscript. Identifies
  the specific reader types who would love your book, grounded in actual scenes and dialogue.
  Runs automatically as part of `/setup-books`, or standalone if needed.

### Commands

- `/setup-books` — The starting point. Reads your manuscript and produces a complete Book
  Analysis: Story Overview, reader avatar profiles, potential hooks, and comp author discovery.
  You confirm what it finds — no manual data entry.

- `/quick-copy` — Fast single-variation ad for a book that's already set up. One paste-ready
  ad block + 2 headlines. For flash sales, new creatives, or when you just need one ad fast.

- `/refresh-copy` — Writes fresh ad copy variations for a book that already has existing copy.
  New hooks, new angles, same static blocks.

### Reference Files

- **Video Hook Strategy Guide** — 23 proven hook strategies with worked examples, advanced
  templates, variation systems, and a scene-type quick reference table
- **TikTok/BookTok Hook Research** — Hook formulas for TikTok, excerpt-over-background video
  format, and Andromeda algorithm considerations
- **Copy Examples** — Full ad copy examples across dark mafia, monster, and contemporary romance
- **Finding Comp Authors** — How to find comp authors through Amazon, Goodreads, BookTok,
  and category research, plus starter lists by subgenre

## Getting Started

1. Install the plugin
2. Run `/setup-books` and provide your manuscript
3. Say "write ad copy for [Book Title]" — it'll produce 5 paste-ready ads

## What You'll Need

- Your manuscript file (.docx, .pdf, .txt, or .md)
- 2-3 strong reader reviews (if published)
- Your ad destination (Shopify, Amazon, retailer page)

That's it. The plugin derives everything else from the manuscript.

## Subgenres Supported

Dark romance, paranormal romance, monster/creature romance, contemporary romance, romantasy,
historical romance, reverse harem, romantic suspense, and more.
