# Example Avatar Profiles — Quality Reference

This is an example of what a finished avatar profiles document looks like. It shows the level
of specificity, manuscript grounding, and reader-language you should aim for. This example
uses a fictional dark vampire romance to illustrate the format.

**Key quality markers to notice:**
- Every bullet in "What draws her in" references a SPECIFIC scene, dialogue line, or character action
- "Who she is" describes a real person with emotional needs, not a marketing segment
- "Tropes she searches for" uses reader language (lowercase, the words people actually type)
- "Best scenes" includes chapter references so you can find them again for ad creatives
- The Tropes Master List at the bottom pulls from ALL avatars into one comprehensive keyword list
- The Themes Master List phrases themes as emotional truths, not genre labels

# Blood & Thorns — Reader Avatar Profiles

**Book:** Blood & Thorns (Standalone)
**Created:** April 2026
**Source:** Full manuscript read-through
**Purpose:** Use these profiles when writing ad copy, headlines, social posts, or any
marketing content. Each avatar represents a real reader type who would love this book.
Match avatars to specific scenes/excerpts when building ads.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

When writing ad copy:
1. Identify which scene/excerpt the creative uses
2. Look at the "Best Scenes" column for each avatar to find which ones connect to that scene
3. Write hooks targeted to those avatars specifically
4. Not every avatar works with every excerpt. That's okay. Pick the ones that fit.

## THE AVATARS

### 1. The Mutual Pining Addict
**Who she is:** She lives for the agony of two people who want each other desperately but
won't make a move. She wants the years of stolen glances, the "I can't have her" internal
monologue, the slow spiral into finally giving in. The longer the pining, the better the payoff.

**What draws her in:**
- Viktor has known Elena is his mate for three years. He's been suffering in silence, watching
  her date other people, because he's afraid telling her the truth about vampires would destroy
  the life she's built.
- She catches herself staring at the way his jaw tightens when she mentions going on a date.
  He catches her staring and looks away first.
- When she finally tells him "I think about you constantly and it's ruining every relationship
  I try to have," he goes completely still. Then he says he's been fighting not to touch her
  since the day they met.
- His second-in-command calls him out: "You've been making decisions with your heart for three
  years and pretending it's strategy. Just tell her."

**Best scenes for this avatar:**
- Elena's confession on the balcony (Chapter 9) where she admits she compares every man to him
- Viktor's silence after her confession, then "It's taking everything I have not to..."
- The first kiss where he traces her jawline like he's memorizing it
- His second telling him he's compromised and Viktor can't deny it (Chapter 14)

**Tropes she searches for:** mutual pining, friends to lovers, slow burn, he's loved her for
years, she doesn't know he's obsessed, finally giving in, slow burn that pays off

---

### 2. The "He Would Burn the World for Her" Reader
**Who she is:** She wants a hero who prioritizes the heroine above everything. Above his empire,
above his duty, above his own survival. The more powerful he is, the more satisfying it is
to watch him crumble for one woman.

**What draws her in:**
- Viktor is a 500-year-old vampire who leads a territory of thousands. He's survived wars,
  killed without hesitation, and built an empire. But the moment Elena is threatened, he
  abandons a critical meeting and runs three miles in under a minute to reach her.
- "The territory can survive without a leader. I can't survive without her."
- He sets up a safe house, stocks it with her favorite foods (including the specific brand of
  tea she drinks), buys her a new phone, and heals her wounds with his blood — all before
  they're even together.
- When he sees bruises from her training, he carries her out of the room, inspects every mark,
  and nearly kills the trainer.

**Best scenes for this avatar:**
- Viktor abandoning the council meeting when he senses she's in danger (Chapter 6)
- "The territory can survive. I cannot." (Chapter 16)
- The safe house scene where he's stocked everything she needs (Chapter 7)
- His reaction to her training injuries — carrying her out, inspecting every bruise (Chapter 16)

**Tropes she searches for:** protective hero, obsessed hero, he would die for her, powerful
hero weak for her, possessive vampire, touch her and die, alpha hero soft for her

---

### 3. The Power Couple Reader
**Who she is:** She's tired of heroines who sit on the sidelines while the hero fights. She
wants the heroine who demands to be trained, argues her way onto the battlefield, and saves
the hero right back. She wants equals.

**What draws her in:**
- Elena doesn't just accept the supernatural world. She demands to be trained. She goes to
  Viktor's weapons master behind his back because she knows Viktor would go easy on her.
- She tells him: "I refuse to be in a relationship where you constantly defend me while I do
  nothing. I want to stand beside you, not behind you."
- She convinces him to let her join the battle not by crying but by logic: "If you trust me,
  let me be there. We're stronger together than apart."
- She saves Viktor and two others from an ambush by using her newly discovered abilities
  offensively for the first time.

**Best scenes for this avatar:**
- Elena telling Viktor she's fighting whether he likes it or not (Chapter 19)
- Her speech to the vampire council where she shuts down the one who questions her (Chapter 20)
- Saving the group from the ambush (Chapter 21)
- Training montage where she progresses from stumbling to lethal (Chapter 18)

**Tropes she searches for:** strong heroine, power couple, she saves him too, badass heroine,
warrior woman, equal partners, fierce heroine, she earns their respect

---

### 4. The Fated Mates Obsessive
**Who she is:** She wants the soulmate bond to be real, binding, and devastating. She needs
the "I knew the moment I saw her" energy. The stakes must be life-and-death — if your mate
rejects you, it kills you.

**What draws her in:**
- Viktor has known Elena is his mate for three years. He's been watching her, protecting her
  from the shadows, unable to tell her why.
- His friend explains the stakes to Elena: "Imagine your worst heartbreak and multiply it by
  a hundred. That's what rejection feels like to our kind. Some don't survive it."
- This isn't hypothetical. A secondary character's mate died because he believed she rejected
  him. She carries his ring on a chain and nearly followed him.
- The mating bond makes Elena immortal. She doesn't just get a partner. She gets eternity.

**Best scenes for this avatar:**
- The mate bond explanation scene (Chapter 8)
- The devastating backstory of the secondary character's lost mate (Chapter 18)
- The bonding scene where Viktor bites Elena and completes their connection (Chapter 17)
- Viktor's internal monologue about the terror of losing her (Chapter 14)

**Tropes she searches for:** fated mates, mate bond, soulmates, vampire mates, one true mate,
she becomes immortal, eternal love, bonding bite, if she dies he dies

## THEMES MASTER LIST

- Choosing to stay when you finally learn the full truth about someone
- The agony of loving someone in silence and the relief of finally saying it
- A man who controls everything losing all control for one woman
- Earning your place in a world that doesn't think you belong
- The line between protection and control in a relationship
- Becoming someone who can kill to protect the people you love, and what that costs you
- Power doesn't come from being fearless. It comes from acting despite the fear.
