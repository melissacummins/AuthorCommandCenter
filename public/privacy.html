Privacy Policy

Author Command Center · Last updated July 15, 2026

Author Command Center is a set of business tools for book authors. This policy describes what information the app stores and how it is used.

Information we store

  • Account information: your email address and authentication credentials, managed by our authentication provider (Supabase).
  • Content you enter: book catalog details, manuscripts, marketing materials, sales records, and other data you create inside the app. It is stored in our database and is visible only to your account.
  • Connected service tokens: if you connect an external service (such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or Shopify), we store the authorization token needed to act on your behalf. Refresh tokens are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and are never exposed to your browser.

Google user data

If you connect Google Drive, the app requests only the drive.file scope, which allows it to see and manage only files it creates itself — it cannot read, browse, or modify anything else in your Drive. The app uses this access for exactly one purpose: uploading files you explicitly choose to export (such as marketing images and videos) to an "Author Command Center" folder in your Drive when you click a Send button.

Author Command Center's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google user data is never sold, never used for advertising, and never shared with third parties, and is not used to train AI models.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties, except the service providers that host the app (e.g. Vercel, Supabase) and services you explicitly connect.
  • We do not use your content for advertising.

AI features

Some features send content you select (for example, manuscript chapters during a scan you start) to the AI provider configured with your own API key. Those requests happen only when you initiate them.

Deleting your data

You can disconnect any external service in Settings at any time, which deletes its stored token. To delete your account and its data, contact us at the email below.
