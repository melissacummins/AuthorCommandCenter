Author Command Center

The all-in-one business toolkit for book authors — write, publish, market, and track your books from one place.

What Author Command Center does

  • Catalog — one source of truth for every book: metadata, tropes, blurbs, keywords, and covers.
  • Writing — draft and manage manuscripts with AI-assisted tools that use your own API keys.
  • Content Creator — turn manuscript moments into marketing hooks, slideshow carousels, annotated excerpt images, and caption videos for TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.
  • Finances — royalty, profit, and transaction tracking across retailers.
  • Operations — inventory, cross-sell analysis, and store upsells for direct sales.
  • Integrations — connect the services you already use, including Shopify, and export finished marketing assets to Google Drive or Dropbox. Google Drive access is limited to files the app creates itself (the drive.file scope) — it never sees the rest of your Drive.
